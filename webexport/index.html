Instructions

goal

The goal of the match is to live as long as possible!

gameplay

Drive your cycle with the keyboard keys. Player 1 turns the cycle with the A and D Keycodes. Player 2 turns his cycle with die arrow keys LEFT and RIGHT.

end of the match

If one of the player crashed into a wall, he dies. It doesn't matter if it is your own wall, the outwall or the wall of your opponent.
